MOVIE/SERIES RANKING TIER LIST

The best TV SERIES/MOVIE tier list on planet earth

BREAKING BAD

Vince Gilligan

Breaking bad is a seemingly perfect drama series that first aired on TV on january 2008. The show explores the way the state of mind of one changes with the decisions one makes in life and much more. Breaking bad is until this day publicly rated as one of the best and most legendary TV series in history.

TRAILER PARK BOYS

Mike Clattenburg

Trailer Park Boys is a low budget yet extremely popular Canadian mockumentary. The show first aired on April 2001 and was discontinued on season 12 episode 10 after the passing of one of the series most notable actors, John Dunsworth.

TOP BOY + TOP BOY:SUMMERHOUSE

William Stefan Smith

This drama takes viewers into the housing estates of east London. There is tension between the drug gangs that operate almost openly and those who strive to live honest lives against the odds in the crime-riddled area. That tension is explored through the interlocking stories of 20-something drug dealer Dushane, who is determined to become the area's Top Boy, and good-natured but vulnerable teenager Ra'Nell, who is forced to grow up quickly following his mother's breakdown and enforced absence.

BLADERUNNER 2049

Denis Villenueve

K, played by Ryan Gosling, is an officer with the Los Angeles Police Department, unearths a secret that could create chaos. He goes in search of a former blade runner who has been missing for over three decades.
